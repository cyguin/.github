# cyguin.

**Drop-in developer tools — open-core, self-hosted, no lock-in.**

(unrelated to https://cygwin.com/. we're not that cool.)

---

Every Cyguin tool installs in minutes, runs on your stack, and ships with a managed tier for teams who'd rather build than operate. Self-hosted where you want control. Managed where you don't. No vendor lock-in, no pricing surprises, no abstraction between you and your data.
